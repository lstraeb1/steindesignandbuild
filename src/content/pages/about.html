WHO WE ARE

Your Trusted Partner In All Things Home Improvement

Stein Design and Build has grown from a small operation into a leading general contracting company in Syracuse. Our founder, Andrew Stein, began working in construction at the age of 14 and has since dedicated his career to crafting high-end, high-quality projects. This deep-rooted passion for building and commitment to excellence has been the driving force behind our success. With each project, we strive to push the boundaries of innovation and creativity, ensuring that our clients receive exceptional results tailored to their unique visions and requirements.

Over the years, we have built a reputation for our meticulous attention to detail, superior craftsmanship, and exceptional client communication. Our extensive experience and dedication to our craft have allowed us to consistently exceed client expectations and deliver projects that not only meet but surpass industry standards. By fostering strong relationships with our clients, we ensure transparency and trust throughout every phase of the project, making the construction process seamless and stress-free.
